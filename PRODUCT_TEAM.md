# Vanta Signal Product Team

This document outlines the roles and responsibilities for the Vanta Signal product team, following the Silicon Valley Product Group (SVPG) model for empowered product teams. Our goal is to operate as a high-performing team, focused on delivering exceptional value to our users.

## Team Structure & Roles

The core product team is a triad of a Product Manager, a Product Designer, and a Tech Lead (Head of Product Engineering), supported by a team of Product Engineers.

| Role                         | Owner         | Responsibilities                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| ---------------------------- | ------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Product Manager**          | *TBD*         | Defines the product vision, strategy, and roadmap. Owns the product backlog, prioritizes features, and is the voice of the customer. Responsible for defining *what* problems to solve and *why*.                                                 |
| **Head of Product Engineering** | *TBD*         | Owns the technical vision, architecture, and execution. Leads the engineering team, ensures code quality, and is responsible for the technical feasibility and delivery of the product. Responsible for *how* the product is built.         |
| **Chief Design Officer**     | *TBD*         | Owns the user experience (UX) and user interface (UI) design. Conducts user research, creates prototypes and high-fidelity designs, and ensures the product is both usable and lovable. Responsible for the overall look and feel. |
| **Product Engineer**         | *Team*        | Builds, tests, and deploys the product. Works in close collaboration with the PM and Designer to implement features, fix bugs, and ensure the product meets the required quality standards.                                                |
| **Forward Deployed Engineer** | *TBD*         | Scopes B2B client problems, diagnoses opportunity vectors, and feeds proto-ideas to design, product, and engineering teams. Acts as the bridge between the customer and the product team.                                                  |

## Operating Model

We will operate in a continuous discovery and delivery model:

*   **Product Discovery:** The Product Manager, Head of Product Engineering, and Chief Design Officer will work together continuously to discover new opportunities and validate ideas. This includes user research, prototyping, and experimentation.
*   **Product Delivery:** The Product Engineers will work in short cycles (sprints) to deliver high-quality, working software. The focus is on shipping value to users as quickly as possible.
*   **Backlog & Roadmap:** The Product Manager will maintain a prioritized product backlog. The roadmap will be a living document, updated regularly based on new learnings and changing priorities.
