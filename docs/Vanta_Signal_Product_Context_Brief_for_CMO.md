# Vanta Signal — Product Context Brief for CMO

**Date:** March 16, 2026
**Author:** Manus AI (as Chief of Product)

---

## 1. BLUF (Bottom Line Up Front)

Vanta Signal has successfully evolved from a prototype into a feature-rich, stable platform for **Personal Operations**. The core intelligence layer is built and the product is now at a strategic inflection point. The focus is shifting from foundational feature development to delivering a powerful, **action-oriented user experience**. 

The key takeaway for marketing is this: the narrative is no longer about *what the product sees*, but about *what the user can do*. User research has unequivocally shown that the most resonant value propositions are **curated control** and **closing the loop from signal to action**. The upcoming product increment is designed to deliver precisely that.

---

## 2. Where We Are: The "Personal Ops" Platform

The platform has matured beyond simple signal aggregation. It is now a cohesive system that helps users manage the complexity of their professional lives. The current build (v0.9.9) delivers on a core promise: to unify a user's scattered communications into a single, intelligent feed.

| Capability | What It Does |
|:---|:---|
| **Unified Intelligence** | Ingests and analyzes signals from five core channels: iMessage, Phone Calls, Zoom, Email, and Calendar. |
| **Relationship Intelligence** | Builds a dynamic map of the user's network, tracks relationship strength, and alerts them when key connections are cooling. |
| **AI-Powered Curation** | Allows users to train the AI by defining custom signal types, setting channel priorities, and reviewing the AI's classifications. |
| **Workflow Automation** | Provides a "no-code" style builder for creating rules that automate responses and actions based on incoming signals. |

This is the foundation upon which the next phase of growth will be built. The platform works, it is stable, and it is rich with data.

---

## 3. What Resonates Most: The User Mandate

Recent user research conducted with MetaLab provided a clear and powerful mandate for the product's direction. The concepts that resonated most strongly were not more data visualizations, but tools that facilitated action and control.

- **The #1 Feature Concept: The Filtered Task List.** Users don't just want to see their signals; they want the platform to extract commitments, follow-ups, and open questions and present them as a curated checklist. This is the 
evolution from a passive dashboard to an active co-pilot.

- **The Killer UX: The Omnipresent Smart Note.** The ability to capture a thought, idea, or to-do item from anywhere, at any time, without breaking workflow, was the second most powerful concept. This transforms the product from a destination you visit into an assistant that is always with you.

---

## 4. What Comes Next: The Three Strategic Moves

Based on this clear user feedback, the next Program Increment is organized around three strategic product moves designed to deliver on this promise of action and control.

1.  **The Action Layer:** This is the highest priority. We are building the features that allow users to act on intelligence directly within the platform. This includes the **Action Items View** (the filtered task list) and surfacing **Quick Actions** (like "Draft Reply" or "Set Reminder") directly on signal cards. This move is about closing the loop.

2.  **The Temporal Layer:** We are evolving the dashboard from a static list of recent events into a time-anchored narrative of the user's day. This includes a **Timeline View** for the main dashboard and a **"What's Ahead"** block that proactively surfaces upcoming meetings and commitments. This move is about providing context and foresight.

3.  **The Identity Layer:** We are making the platform more personal and adaptive. This includes **Mode-Driven Layouts** that change the dashboard based on the user's current context (e.g., "Executive" vs. "Creative" mode) and building a unified **"My Rules"** interface for users to customize their personal AI. This move is about delivering a bespoke experience.

---

## 5. What This Means for Marketing

This strategic shift provides a powerful new narrative for marketing and brand positioning.

- **Shift the Messaging from "See Everything" to "Do Anything."** The core marketing message should evolve. It is not just about the power of the intelligence layer, but the leverage it provides. The key benefit is not just knowing, but *doing*, faster and more effectively.

- **Target the "Overwhelmed Operator."** The ideal customer profile is the high-output professional drowning in a sea of multi-channel communication. The pain point is not a lack of information, but a lack of a system to manage it. Vanta Signal is that system.

- **Lean into "Curation" and "Control" as Keywords.** The brand voice should emphasize empowerment. This is not another rigid, opinionated productivity tool. It is a flexible, personal system that adapts to the user, putting them in control of their own operational rhythm.

- **Prepare for a v1.0 Launch.** The successful execution of this next PI will result in a product that is feature-complete and ready for a broader market launch. The marketing strategy should begin to align around a v1.0 release in Q2 2026, with messaging and campaigns that reflect this new, action-oriented value proposition.
