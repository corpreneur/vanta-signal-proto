# Vanta Signal -- Unified Assessment (v2.0)

**Date:** March 13, 2026

---

## 1. Executive Summary

The platform has undergone a radical transformation in just 48 hours, evolving from a simple prototype into a functional, multi-channel signal intelligence platform. The pace of innovation is extraordinary. However, this rapid growth has come at the cost of stability and security. All three review teams (Product, Engineering, and Strategy) have arrived at the same conclusion: the platform must immediately enter a **stabilization sprint** to address critical P0 bugs and security risks before any further feature development.

## 2. The Mandate: Stabilize Before Expanding

The consensus is clear: the "move fast and break things" phase is over. The platform is now a product, and it must be treated as such. The next two weeks will be dedicated to a stabilization sprint. The only items on the backlog are the P0 and P1 findings from the Product and Engineering reviews. No new features. No new channels. The goal is to create a stable, secure, and demo-ready platform.

## 4. Strategic Roadmap

The long-term strategic path, as defined by the Chief of Staff and ratified by the Senior Partner, is to pursue **Option 3: Feature of Vanta Wireless**. This provides the most direct path to market and creates a powerful differentiator for the core Vanta Wireless offering. Architecturally, we will continue to preserve **Option 2: Standalone SaaS** as a viable future path.

## 5. Conclusion

The Vanta Signal platform has demonstrated immense potential. The team has proven its ability to ship complex, user-centric features at an incredible velocity. The next two weeks are about matching that velocity with the discipline and rigor required to build a world-class product. Stabilize the foundation. Then, and only then, build the next floor.
